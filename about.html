Our Story

Born from frustration, built for results

So we built something different — a team that codes, designs, writes, and strategizes under one roof, with radical transparency and a results-first mindset.

Today, we've grown to a 20+ person team across India and the US, serving clients in 12 industries — and we're just getting started.
